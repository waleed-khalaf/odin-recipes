Grilled Cheese

Home

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a warm, crispy, and gooey comfort food made by layering slices of cheese between two pieces of buttered bread, then toasting or grilling until the bread is golden brown and the cheese melts into a creamy filling.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Mayonaise (optional)

Steps

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Put cheese into the bread
  3. Cook the sandiwch over medium-low heat
  4. Flip after 2-4mins and repeat
  5. Serve
