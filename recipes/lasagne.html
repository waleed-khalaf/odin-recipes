Description

Lasagna is an Italian baked pasta dish made by layering wide, flat sheets of pasta with rich sauces—typically a tomato-based meat sauce and creamy béchamel—along with cheese, often mozzarella and Parmesan, then baking until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Toamato sauce
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Make the Bechamel Sauce
  3. Cook the pasta sheets
  4. Assemble the lasanga
  5. Bake
  6. Rest & Serve
